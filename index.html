sva: (sanskrit: self/own)
gram (sanskrit: village)

Each person builds their abode with dreams that reflect their unique identity, purpose, design, space, and patterns. In crafting these homes, they don't just build structures on plots of land; they cultivate an entire ecosystem, welcoming diverse flora and fauna. Together, they nurture an ambiance that echoes the balance and harmony of a village.

We hold the belief that such homes cannot be solely envisioned by architects. They emerge through a collaborative process, where the client's aspirations meld seamlessly with our expertise and craftsmanship.
